This work has been developed by Naim Al-Zaben and Dr. Zoltán Cseresnyés from the Applied Systems Biology research group of the Hans Knöll Institute Jena, as analyzing tool to ACAQ-v4 (Algorithim for Confrontation Assays Quantification 4) framework.

The research group Applied Systems Biology is concerned with the mathematical modelling and computer simulation of infection processes caused by human-pathogenic fungi. The spatio-temporal data basis for these models is acquired by automatizing the data analysis of microscopy images on infection processes for high-throughput scanning.
